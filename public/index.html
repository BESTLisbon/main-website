A little about us...

BEST Lisboa is one of the 90 BEST Local groups that exist all over Europe. We are a non governmental, apolitical organization that has the mission of Empowering Diversity and Developing Students. Our 5 values: flexibility, friendship, fun, progress and learning are the principles that guide our members also known as BESTies.

Our events

BEST organises a set of events that mainly focus on providing unique experiences to technology students and on complementing their education in the perspective that these experiences would help them in their future career path. Furthermore, BEST events also have a very strong international component that cultivates a spirit of integration and diversity.
